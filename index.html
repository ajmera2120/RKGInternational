RKG INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

RKG International School is an unaided English medium school Educational Society and affiliated to C.B.S.E Board New Delhi. Our school campus provides a serene, peaceful & eco-friendly atmosphere & natural surroundings full of greenery. The aroma of purity, austerity, tranquility & divinity prevailes all through, mixed with the fragrance of the blossoming flowers. Lush green lawns, playground, children's park equipped with all playing equipments & various rides and eye-catching gardens are the main attractions of the institution.
